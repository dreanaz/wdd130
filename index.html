Hawai'i|Northshore

The State of Hawai'i includes eight islands: Ni'ihau, Kaua'i, Moloka'i, Kaho'olawe, Maui, Lana'i, Hawai'i and O'ahu. It is known as the Aloha State, for it's beautiful beaches, scenery and aloha spirit. Hawai'i is also known for chocolate covered macadamia nuts, pineapple fields and Merry Monarch Hula competition to name a few. The island of O'ahu is well-known for great surf up on the Northshore. Many tourist and locals love to watch the sunset with family and friends.
